About Usanii Mashariki

Usanii Mashariki Gallery is dedicated to showcasing the rich artistic heritage of East Africa. We provide a platform for both established and emerging artists to share their work with the world.

Our mission is to preserve, promote, and celebrate East African art while fostering cultural exchange and artistic growth within our community.
